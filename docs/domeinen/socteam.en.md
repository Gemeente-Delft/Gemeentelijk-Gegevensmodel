# Social Teams

Like the other models in the social domain, the data model for social teams is built around the [*Client*](socdomeingeneriek.en.md) object type. Social-team service delivery is based on the ZRM (Self-reliance Matrix). In the social-teams model, the ZRM is elaborated into criteria and levels on which the client may score. Client scores are recorded in *ZRM Score per Criterion* and totalled in *ZRM Score*; a client may have multiple over time.

Based on these scores, a *Plan of Approach* is drawn up for the client.
