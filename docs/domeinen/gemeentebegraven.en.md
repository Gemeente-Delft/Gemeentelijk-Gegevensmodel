# Municipal Burials

A *Municipal Funeral* is linked to a *Natural Person*, since the municipal duty extends beyond people who are formally registered. It also occurs that, for example, foreigners receive a municipal funeral. The figure below shows that a *NATURAL PERSON* may have a *Municipal Funeral*.
